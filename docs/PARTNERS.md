## Analytika / Mastrin Digital Solutions

**Role:** AI & Software Development | **Location:** Canada

### About

Analytika (dba Mastrin) is a technology company developing end-to-end Industry 4.0 solutions based on AI, Machine Learning, and IoT for manufacturing, oil & gas, agriculture, and smart buildings. They are responsible for the AI-powered recipe creation engine that makes the system truly autonomous.

### Project Responsibilities

- Generative AI model development for PLC recipe creation
- Computer vision algorithms (CNN, GD&T, Stereo Vision)
- Camera module development (Intel RealSense for R&D; industrial camera for production)
- PLC API module (interface between AI and production equipment)
- Edge computing module (on-premise AI inference)
- Digital Twin Control Tower
